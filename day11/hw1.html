My 5 favorite movies

1. 3 Idiots (Bollywood)

"3 Idiots" is a Bollywood masterpiece that explores the flaws of the education system in India while highlighting the importance of passion and creativity. The story follows three engineering students and their journey through college, friendship, and self-realization. With humor, emotion, and powerful messages, the film encourages students to follow their dreams rather than blindly chase grades.

2. Inception (Hollywood)

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that dives into the complex world of dreams. The film revolves around a group of specialists who enter people’s subconscious minds to plant ideas. Known for its intelligent storytelling, visual effects, and layered plot, "Inception" challenges viewers to think deeply about reality and perception.

3. Dangal (Bollywood)

"Dangal" is based on the real-life story of Indian wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat and his daughters Geeta and Babita. The film showcases their struggle, discipline, and determination to become world-class wrestlers despite societal pressures. It is a powerful tale of empowerment, especially for women in sports, and celebrates dedication and family support.

4. The Shawshank Redemption (Hollywood)

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a timeless Hollywood classic about hope, friendship, and resilience. The film follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly imprisoned for murder, who overcomes years of harsh prison life through intelligence and inner strength. It’s an emotionally rich story that shows how perseverance and hope can lead to freedom.

5. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara (Bollywood)

"Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara" is a heartwarming film about three friends who go on a road trip in Spain that changes their lives forever. It beautifully explores themes like love, fear, friendship, and living in the moment. Through its scenic visuals, meaningful dialogue, and soul-stirring music, the film encourages viewers to cherish every moment and follow their hearts.
